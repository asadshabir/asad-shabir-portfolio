OG Image Generator

This page shows how your branded OG image should look. Export instructions:

  1. Open this page in Chrome/Firefox
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools
  3. Click the OG Preview tab below
  4. Right-click the image → Save image as...
  5. Save as og-image.jpg in the public/ folder
  6. Or use browser screenshot tool at 1200×630 for best results

Your OG image should be 1200×630px for optimal social sharing.
